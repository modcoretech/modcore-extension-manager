        Overall Score

        How scores are calculated
        • Each permission has a base Security and Privacy impact sourced from permissions.json.
        • High-risk and critical permissions carry extra weight and bypass diminishing returns.
        • Having many permissions adds a small sprawl penalty.
        • Security & Privacy scores are combined - the lower one is weighted 60 %, the higher 40 %.
        • The final score maps to a risk category: Excellent → High Risk.
