# Contributing to modcore Extension Manager

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **modcore Extension Manager**!  
Whether it’s fixing bugs, suggesting new features, improving documentation, or just giving feedback, your help is greatly appreciated.

---

## How You Can Contribute

- **Report issues or bugs:** Let us know if something isn’t working as expected. Clear descriptions help us fix problems faster.  
- **Suggest features or improvements:** Have an idea for a new feature or a better way of doing things? Share it with us.  
- **Improve documentation:** Help us make guides, explanations, or instructions easier to understand.  
- **Create themes or add-ons:** Since modcore supports extra content, feel free to propose or create fun enhancements.  

> Small contributions are always welcome. Don’t worry if it feels minor - it all helps!

---

## Guidelines & Best Practices

- **Respect privacy and security:** Do not introduce anything that could track users or compromise their data.  
- **Keep it clear and understandable:** Write things in a way that others can follow easily.  
- **Be respectful and professional:** Constructive discussions help everyone, and make the project a better place to contribute.  
- **Focus on quality:** Whether it’s code, documentation, or ideas, aim for clarity, accuracy, and usefulness.
